Services


  • Residential Care: Our modern and comfortable facilities offer round-the-clock care for seniors who require assistance with daily activities or specialized medical support.

  • Dementia Care: We understand the unique challenges faced by individuals living with dementia. Our dedicated dementia care program provides tailored support to enhance quality of life and promote independence.

  • Respite Care: Family caregivers need rest and support too. Our respite care services offer temporary relief, allowing caregivers to take a break while ensuring their loved ones receive high-quality care in a safe and welcoming environment.

  • Palliative Care: Compassionate end-of-life care is provided by our skilled team, focusing on pain management, comfort, and dignity for residents and their families during this sensitive time.


Why Choose Us


  • Experienced and Compassionate Staff: Our team of skilled professionals is passionate about making a difference in the lives of seniors. With extensive training and a genuine commitment to care, we ensure that residents receive the attention and support they deserve.

  • Safe and Comfortable Environment: iHome Care is designed to feel like home. From cozy living spaces to beautiful gardens, we prioritize the safety, comfort, and well-being of our residents.

  • Community and Engagement: We believe in the importance of social connection and meaningful activities. Our diverse range of programs and events encourage residents to stay active, engaged, and connected with others.

  • Family-Centered Care: We understand that family plays a vital role in the care journey. We welcome families as partners, providing support, guidance, and regular communication to ensure peace of mind for everyone involved.
